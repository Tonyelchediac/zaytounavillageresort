About Us
Welcome to Zaytouna Village Resort, your serene getaway nestled along the mountains of Batroun. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea and indulge in a wide range of amenities and activities. From luxurious accommodations to diverse dining options and an extensive list of on-site experiences, we invite you to unwind and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of our coastal retreat.
